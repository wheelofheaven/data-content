## Why this tradition is in the corpus

The Hebrew Bible is the single densest repository of the motifs the
Wheel of Heaven frame reads as compressed memory of Elohim contact:
creation by "the Powerful Ones," Nephilim as giant offspring of the
Watchers and human women, a global reset by flood, covenant as legal
framework between creators and created, and cosmic order keyed to
celestial cycles. The Raëlian canon anchors its own reading of
origins in this corpus directly — see the
[Elohim concept hub](/explore/concept/elohim/) for the textual spine.
