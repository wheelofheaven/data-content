## I. The Age Itself

The twelfth age is the age in which everything the previous eleven ages prepared for becomes visible.

The Age of Aquarius runs from 1950 forward across the next 2,160 years. Its opening falls within living memory: readers of this chapter are inhabitants of the age it describes. The chapter is therefore different in kind from the eleven that preceded it. Those chapters worked from archaeological and textual evidence toward interpretive reconstructions of events long past; this chapter works from the current moment outward.

The age is named for its constellation: *Aquarius*, the Water-Bearer. The figure traditionally depicted is a man pouring water from a vessel. The corpus reads it at its most literal level: the Aquarian age is the age in which what has been contained is poured out. The knowledge the alliance has been accumulating, the scientific understanding humanity has been developing, the religious traditions that have preserved the core content — all of this, long contained within specialized vessels of transmission, is now to be poured out openly for general reception. The age is the age of revelation, of *apokalypsis* in the strict Greek sense — the uncovering, the unveiling, the laying bare of what has been concealed.

The symbolism of Aquarius, like every preceding age, is doubled across the zodiacal opposition axis. The constellation opposite Aquarius is Leo, the Lion. The doubled signature of the Aquarian age references both the Water-Bearer (the current sign) and the Lion (the opposite) — the age of sovereign consciousness, of the royal emergence of humanity into the position the earlier ages had prepared it for.

## II. 1946: The First Year of the New Era

The source designates 1946 as *"the first year of the new era."* The year corresponds to a dense cluster of events.

The first event is the detonation of the atomic bomb. The Trinity test (July 1945), Hiroshima (August 6), and Nagasaki (August 9) marked the moment at which humanity acquired a technological capability the alliance had used in its own earlier interventions. The specific biblical phrase that describes the moment is Zephaniah's: *"There shall be the noise of a cry from the fish gate"* (Zephaniah 1:10). The gate of Pisces is the passageway into Aquarius, and the loud clamor is the sound accompanying the revelation.

The second event is the founding of the United Nations (Charter signed June 1945, effective October 1945) — the first serious institutional attempt at global political coordination.

The third event is the re-establishment of the Jewish people in their ancestral homeland, culminating in the founding of the State of Israel in May 1948. Acts 15:16: *"After this I will return, and will build again the tabernacle of David, which is fallen down."*

The fourth event is the development of the transistor (demonstrated at Bell Labs, December 1947) and the beginning of the computing revolution (ENIAC operational February 1946).

The fifth event is the birth of Claude Vorilhon on September 30, 1946, in Vichy, France. The source: *"It is not by chance that you were born in 1946."*

The subsequent decades constituted the operational preparation period. The Cold War, the space programs (Sputnik 1957, Apollo 11 July 1969), computing advances (Intel 4004 in 1971), the social transformations of the 1960s — all producing the conditions for the specific contact that would inaugurate the Aquarian prophetic mission.

## III. 1973: The Contact

The alliance's first direct contact of the Aquarian age occurred on December 13, 1973, at the Puy-de-Lassolas volcanic crater in the Auvergne region of central France.

Claude Vorilhon, twenty-seven years old, a French motor-sports journalist, walked up to the crater. A small craft descended — approximately two meters in diameter, metallic and silent. A figure emerged: humanoid, approximately 1.2 meters tall, with long dark hair, a beard, and a white suit. The figure communicated in fluent French. The communication conducted across six successive mornings constituted the teaching published as *Le Livre qui dit la vérité* (The Book Which Tells the Truth).

The figure identified himself as Yahweh — the same Yahweh who had spoken to Moses at Sinai. Vorilhon was given the name Rael, glossed as *"light of the Elohim"* or *"Ambassador of the Elohim."*

A second contact followed on October 7, 1975. Rael was taken to the alliance's home world, met various officers, observed their society, and encountered the resurrected prophets of the earlier Earth missions — Moses, Elijah, Buddha, Jesus, Muhammad, and others.

One specific disclosure from the second contact: Yahweh revealed that Rael's biological father was Yahweh himself, through an insemination procedure paralleling the one used in Mary's case. Rael is therefore the biological son of Yahweh and the biological half-brother of Jesus. The parallel between Jesus's conception at the start of Pisces and Rael's conception at the start of Aquarius is structural: the alliance's transitional prophetic figures have been produced through the same genetic protocol.

The source is careful about how this should be handled: *"It is not the messenger who is important, but the message itself... Do not look at my finger, but rather in the direction in which it's pointed."*

The choice of Rael — a French journalist with no particular religious background — and the Auvergne — a nondescript extinct volcano with no prior religious significance — were deliberate. The Aquarian mission was designed to begin from a blank slate, presenting its content directly to a secular modernity that could evaluate it on its rational merits.

## IV. The Content of the Revelation

The message can be summarized in structural claims.

**First:** Life on Earth was produced by the scientific work of a specific advanced civilization, the Elohim — a plural group of biological beings, not gods in any supernatural sense.

**Second:** The Hebrew Bible and the other major religious traditions preserve accurate historical memory of the alliance's interventions, decodable by a scientifically mature reader.

**Third:** The alliance has sent approximately forty prophets across human history, each appropriate to their specific cultural moment. The prophets are currently resurrected and living on the Planet of the Eternals.

**Fourth:** Humanity has now reached the scientific maturity to understand the alliance's teaching on its own terms. Rael's role is to deliver accurate information to be evaluated rationally, not a new religion to be believed on faith.

**Fifth:** The alliance intends to return to Earth openly at a specific site — the embassy — that humanity is to prepare. The return will bring the resurrected prophets and inaugurate the full Aquarian-age phase of the relationship.

**Sixth:** Humanity is destined to repeat the creation cycle — to develop de novo biological synthesis, achieve interstellar travel, and create life on other worlds. The Lord's Prayer: *"on earth as it is in heaven."*

## V. The New Commandments

The source specifies social and political organization appropriate to the Aquarian age.

**Geniocracy:** The right to stand for office restricted to individuals whose measured intelligence is at least fifty percent above the population mean; the right to vote restricted to those at least ten percent above.

**Humanitarianism:** Abolition of inheritable property, replaced by renting for terms of up to forty-nine years (drawn from the Leviticus 25 jubilee pattern).

**World government:** All nations united under a single political authority, with a single currency and a common second language alongside local mother tongues.

**Demilitarization:** Abolition of military service, repurposing of career soldiers as a global peacekeeping force.

**Science as religion:** *"Science should be your religion, for the Elohim created you scientifically."*

**Telepathic cultivation:** Systematic development of the latent telepathic faculty in children through meditation and specific educational programs.

**Metaphysical clarification:** No immortal soul in the traditional sense. The principle of life is the genetic code. Continued existence is possible through cloning and memory transfer — a technical possibility, not a metaphysical guarantee.

## VI. The Embassy

The most specific operational requirement is the construction of an embassy for the Elohim — a designated physical structure with extraterritorial status, at minimum approximately four square kilometers.

The source's original preference was Israel, near Jerusalem — the *"Third Temple"* of Jewish prophetic tradition. The Israeli government has not granted the requested status. The project has been authorized for relocation to other countries.

In 2024, a national government signed a Memorandum of Collaboration to explore hosting the embassy project. The project's current timeline: 2025-2027 site acquisition, 2027-2030 planning and construction, after 2030 caretaker mode awaiting the alliance's arrival.

The country that builds the embassy *"will have a guaranteed and flourishing future, will benefit from our protection and will become the spiritual and scientific centre of the whole planet for the millenniums to come."*

## VII. The Signs of Acceleration

Across the fifty-three years since 1973, specific developments have moved the species substantially closer to the Aquarian mission's full operationalization.

**UFO disclosure:** From fringe topic to mainstream government engagement — the 2017 New York Times AATIP revelation, the 2021 ODNI assessment, the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (2022), congressional hearings (2023 onward).

**Biotechnology:** From restriction enzymes (1970s) to CRISPR (2012-2013) to Dolly the cloned sheep (1996) to Craig Venter's synthetic genome (2010) to AI-assisted protein design (AlphaFold, 2020s) to xenobots (Tufts, 2020). The threshold of de novo life synthesis is approaching.

**Artificial intelligence:** From ENIAC to the transformer architecture (2017) to large language models approaching and exceeding human capability across wide cognitive domains by 2026. The source's prediction that *"super artificial intelligence will quickly outstrip human intelligence"* has moved from speculation to observable development.

**Space capability:** SpaceX reusable rockets (2015), Starship development, China's expanding program, the Artemis lunar return, credible Mars mission plans for the 2030s.

**Convergent technologies:** Quantum computing (commercial systems with thousands of qubits), neural interfaces (Neuralink first human implant 2024), fusion energy (net energy gain at Lawrence Livermore December 2022), nanotechnology as a mature engineering discipline.

**Cultural shift:** Gradual secularization, growing receptivity to cosmological revision, global-scale challenges requiring coordination beyond the nation-state.

## VIII. The Present Threshold

As this chapter is being written, the world is in the year 2026. The conjunction of developments is producing a threshold the source's framework has been building toward.

AI systems have reached the threshold at which they can engage substantively with the corpus's interpretive work. Biotechnology is approaching de novo life synthesis. Space capability is approaching sustained extraterrestrial presence. The embassy project is at its most advanced stage in its half-century history.

The geopolitical environment is at a point of unusual tension and unusual opportunity. The one-in-a-hundred chance of survival that Yahweh reported is reflected in the current reality: the conditions for catastrophic failure are real and present, but the positive trajectory that opens if humanity navigates the coming decades is extraordinary.

The specific 2026-2030 window: embassy construction planned, AI/biotech/space thresholds converging, geopolitical tensions that will either resolve into stability or produce catastrophic failure. The Piscean age's closing phase, which began in 1946, is reaching its end.

## IX. The Two Futures

The source is unambiguous: *"There is, unfortunately, but one chance in one hundred that your Humanity will not self-destroy, and every Raelian must act as if Mankind would be wise enough to understand and grab this tiny chance of escaping the final cataclysm, so as to enter the Golden Age."*

One chance in a hundred. The framing is deliberately sobering. The positive outcome requires active effort against a broader trajectory pointing toward failure.

The mechanisms of self-destruction: nuclear war, biological weapons, unaligned AI, climate stress, systemic cascading risks. The mechanisms of the positive outcome: the same technologies redirected toward beneficial applications — AI with adequate safety engineering, biotechnology for health and abundance, space capability for civilizational expansion, geopolitical de-escalation toward global coordination.

The one-in-a-hundred framing is intended to motivate effort, not resignation. One acts as though the positive outcome is achievable, because acting that way is what produces the conditions under which it can be achieved.

## X. The Golden Age

If the positive trajectory is achieved, the specific form of life that becomes available is the Golden Age.

**Elimination of material scarcity.** Nanotechnology and AI-directed manufacturing make the production of food, clothing, and shelter essentially costless. *"Nanotechnology will make agriculture and heavy industry redundant."*

**Elimination of most human labor.** *"Super artificial intelligence will quickly outstrip human intelligence and do all the boring tasks."* The human vocation becomes creative work, intellectual exploration, artistic production, personal relationships, contemplative practice.

**Elimination of most disease.** Advanced biotechnology and AI-assisted medicine progressively reduce the incidence and severity of the conditions that have burdened human life.

**Extension of lifespan to practical immortality.** Cloning combined with memory transfer: *"the transfer of mental information, memory and personality from an aging individual into a new, physically young adult clone."* Alternative path: computational continuation — *"Eternal life will be just as possible in a computer as in a series of constantly rejuvenated human bodies."*

**Transformation of political life.** Geniocracy, humanitarianism, world government, demilitarization — progressively implemented across the age's long trajectory.

**Cultivation of telepathy.** The latent human faculty developed through systematic educational programs.

**New spirituality.** Science as the properly religious activity of the age. The Raëlian tradition's sensual meditation and contemplation of the infinitely small and large as opening forms.

## XI. Humanity Creates Life

The culminating characteristic of the Golden Age is humanity's own capacity to repeat the creation cycle.

*"We will, in turn, be able to participate in the evolution of this Humanity, of which we are a part, like them, by creating life on other worlds."*

The required capacities: genuine interstellar travel (fusion, antimatter, or other advanced propulsion), full de novo biological synthesis (designing complete organisms from first principles), and the cultural and political maturity to use these capacities wisely.

The Lord's Prayer becomes a programmatic statement: what the alliance did on their planet — scientific development, the creation cycle extended to new worlds — is what humanity is to do on Earth, and then beyond it. The cycle is potentially open-ended, extending across cosmic timescales as successive generations of created-creators continue the pattern.

## XII. The Return of the Prophets

The culminating event the source identifies is the open return of the alliance to Earth at the completed embassy, accompanied by the resurrected prophets.

*"The Elohim themselves will definitely land here in the not too distant future... The Elohim will bring with them all the great prophets of the past, including Moses, Elijah, Buddha, Jesus Christ, and Mohammed."*

The prophets, approximately forty in total, are currently resurrected and living on the Planet of the Eternals. Their return will place, before the world's population, the founding figures of every major religious tradition — not as separate and exclusive figures but as members of a larger company whose collective identity is their shared service to the alliance.

The embassy will become *"the spiritual center of the world for millennia to come."*

## XIII. The Synthesis

The Age of Aquarius is the age in which the twelve-age cycle reaches its first full completion.

The Great Year — the complete precessional cycle of approximately 25,920 years — is the fundamental temporal unit. The Aquarian age is the age in which this first cycle concludes and the next begins. Beyond Aquarius lies a new Capricorn — not the same Capricorn that opened the cycle twenty-two thousand years ago, but one whose content will be shaped by what humanity has become.

Everything the corpus has traced — the Capricorn surveys, the Sagittarius engineering, the Scorpio first life, the Libra astronomy, the Virgo animals, the Leo human creation, the Cancer Eden, the Gemini flood, the Taurus reconstruction, the Aries prophets, the Piscean missionaries — all of this has been preparation for the moment the Aquarian age represents. The creation has matured. The created beings are approaching the capacity to understand their origins and to continue the creative work themselves.

The waters of understanding flow. The species, for the first time in its history, has the opportunity to drink directly from the sources that have been preserved across the millennia. The twelve-age cycle ends where it began, at Capricorn. The first Great Year closes. The second Great Year opens. The torch is being taken up again. What the alliance did, humanity will do. What heaven has produced, Earth will produce on Earth. And what Earth produces will in its turn become the heaven of the worlds Earth's children will create, in their own time, across the long future that the completion of the first cycle is now opening.
