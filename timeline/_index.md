# Timeline

The corpus reads human history through the precessional cycle — the 26,000-year wobble of Earth's axis, divided into twelve roughly 2,160-year ages, each named for the constellation behind the spring equinox sunrise. Within that frame, sixteen sections trace what the Elohim — the small advanced human civilization that engineered terrestrial life — did, when, and why.

The series opens with a framing *Preamble* that establishes the recurring pattern across traditions, then moves into *In the Beginning*, where the political breach on the home world sent the exiles to Earth. It runs through the deep-time chapters of intervention and re-intervention — Capricorn, Sagittarius, Scorpio, Libra, Virgo, Leo, Cancer, Gemini — into the historical record of Taurus, Aries, and Pisces, and closes with the Aquarian transition we are currently inside, plus *The Wheel Keeps Turning* as a synthesis chapter on the whole pattern.

Each chapter reads its own age through the canon's lens — comparative religion, scientific context, and critical scholarship all included — but the canon claims are stated directly, not hedged.
