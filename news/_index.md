# Newsroom

The Newsroom is where we read **current events** through the canon. Each entry — a *Dispatch* — anchors to something that happened: a discovery, an announcement, an anniversary, a cultural moment. The angle is what the canon makes of it.

## Dispatches vs Explainers

Dispatches are short, dated, and decay. They aren't trying to be definitive — they're trying to be *current*. If you want the evergreen, thesis-driven treatment of a topic, see [Articles](/articles/), where Explainers do the slow work.

A Dispatch can seed a future Explainer; an Explainer never gets reduced to a Dispatch.

## What you won't find here

- Breaking-news aggregation. We aren't trying to be a wire service.
- Sensational framing. Same scholarly register as the rest of the site.
- Proselytizing. Dispatches invite inquiry; they don't conclude for the reader.
