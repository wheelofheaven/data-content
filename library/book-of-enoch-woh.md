**1 Enoch (Book of the Watchers, chs 1-36) — Wheel of Heaven
Translation.** The most directly WoH-engaged text in the entire
pseudepigrapha — named in *Yes... I am Raelian* among the texts
"closer to the truth," alongside the Mahābhārata, Kojiki, Popol Vuh,
and the Epic of Gilgamesh.

**248 verses** across 36 chapters, produced from a **per-verse
layered witness reconstruction**: Aramaic Dead Sea Scrolls fragments
(**4Q201 / 4Q202 / 4Q204 / 4Q205 / 4Q206 / 4Q207**; late 3rd c. BCE
through 1st c. BCE — the OLDEST surviving Enoch material) as the
PRIMARY witness where preserved; **Greek Akhmim Panopolitanus codex**
(6th c. CE; chs 1-32 substantially complete) as the SECONDARY
witness; **Ge'ez (Ethiopic)** — the only complete witness, but the
most-removed in time and language — as the TERTIARY witness and
fallback. The Greek Akhmim breaks off mid-chapter 32; chs 33-36 are
witnessed by Ge'ez alone.

**Sources:** Milik 1976 *The Books of Enoch* (the foundational
publication of the Qumran Aramaic fragments) — THE modern critical
edition for the Aramaic witness; Black 1985 *The Book of Enoch or 1
Enoch* (Greek + commentary); Nickelsburg 2001 *1 Enoch 1* (Hermeneia,
chs 1-36, 81-108) — THE standard modern critical commentary; Charles
1912 *The Book of Enoch* (PD, Ge'ez baseline); Knibb 1978 *The
Ethiopic Book of Enoch* (Ge'ez scholarly standard); Tigchelaar–García
Martínez DJD XXXVI (2000); VanderKam 1984 *Enoch and the Growth of an
Apocalyptic Tradition*; Reed 2005 *Fallen Angels and the History of
Judaism and Christianity*; Stuckenbruck 2007 + 2014.

**Narrative arc.** Enoch's opening theophany prophecy (chs 1-5; the
*Jude 14-15 quote site* at 1:9); the Watchers' descent on Mount
Hermon (ch 6) + the 200 sons-of-heaven taking human wives + the 20
Watcher chiefs (Šemiḥazah, ʿAzazʾēl, Bărāqʾēl, Kōkabʾēl, etc.);
the engendering of the Nephilim giants (ch 7); **Asael's
knowledge-transmission catalog** — metallurgy, weapons-craft,
cosmetics, magic, astronomical-divination (ch 8); the archangels'
intercession (ch 9); the Most High's commission + Asael bound at
Dudael (ch 10); **Enoch the scribe-intercessor** (chs 12-13); **the
throne-vision and the Great Glory** (ch 14 — the four-tradition
throne-vision cluster with Ezek 1 + Dan 7 + Shi'ur Qomah + Rev
1+4); the indictment-sealed + the **giants-become-evil-spirits
demonology** (chs 15-16); Enoch's cosmic tours through the abyss +
mountains-of-fire + **the seven mountains of God** (chs 17-19, 24-25);
the **list of seven archangels** (ch 20; Aramaic 4 vs Greek 7 textual
variant preserved); the prison of the fallen stars (ch 21); **the
four hollow places of the dead** (ch 22 — the foundational
intermediate-state doctrine); the **tree of life on the paradisiacal
mountain** (chs 24-25); **Jerusalem as the center of the earth** (ch
26); **the Valley of Hinnom = Gehenna** (ch 27 — the fountainhead text
for the 12 NT *geenna* references); the eastern paradise + aromatic
trees (chs 28-31); **the tree of wisdom = Genesis 3 tree of knowledge**
(ch 32 — Raphael's identification; the Sophia/Hokhmah tradition
fountainhead); the four-corners gates of heaven (chs 33-36; Ge'ez
witness only).

**The Watchers myth.** The 200 sons-of-heaven (*bnei elohim*; Aramaic
*bn-ilm*; Greek *huioi tou theou*) descend under Šemiḥazah to take
human wives (1 En 6-7) and beget the Nephilim — directly expanding
{% libref(book="genesis", chapter=6, verse=1) %}Genesis 6:1-4{% end %} into a fully-developed
mythological narrative. Asael (Aramaic ʿAśāʾēl; Greek + Ge'ez
Azazel) teaches humanity metallurgy, swords, knives, shields,
breastplates, mining, jewelry, cosmetics, alchemy, and astrology (1 En
8:1-3) — **the canon's central Enoch citation site**. The
Watchers-as-Elohim-civilization reading and the
Asael-as-Elohim-technology-transfer reading are preserved as
canonical-reception phenomena at the **apparatus level only**
(glossary entries + per-verse commentary); the translation surface
preserves the scholarly mainstream reading (Reed 2005 *Fallen Angels*;
Nickelsburg 2001 Hermeneia; Stuckenbruck 2014). Same
acknowledgment-without-endorsement framework as the established
Anunnaki + Igigi + apkallū precedents in the Mesopotamian sub-corpus.

**Translation choices preserved:**

- **Per-verse layered witness attribution** (Aramaic primary + Greek
  secondary + Ge'ez tertiary) — the project's first deployment of
  this schema.
- **Aramaic onomastics preserved** per Milik 1976 4Q201/4Q204:
  Šemiḥazah, ʿAśāʾēl, Bărāqʾēl, Kōkabʾēl, Šamšīʾēl, Šaḥrīʾēl,
  Mīkāʾēl, Śarīʾēl, Rāpāʾēl, Gabrīʾēl, ʾŪrīʾēl, Rĕʿūʾēl. The Greek
  + Ge'ez assimilated forms (Azazel for ʿAśāʾēl; Sariel for Śarīʾēl)
  appear where the Greek or Ge'ez is the witness.
- **Aramaic 4 vs Greek 7 archangels** textual variant preserved at
  ch 20 (Aramaic 4Q204 preserves only 4: Michael, Sariel, Raphael,
  Gabriel; Greek + Ge'ez expand to 7: Uriel, Raphael, Raguel,
  Michael, Saraqael, Gabriel, Remiel).
- **300-vs-3000 cubits giant-stature** variant preserved at ch 7:2
  (Aramaic 4Q201 reads 300 cubits; Greek + Ge'ez read 3000 cubits).
- *Watchers* renders Aramaic ʿīrīn / Greek *egrēgoroi* / Ge'ez
  *teguh* — the scholarly standard English convention.
- *Gehenna* at ch 27 preserves the Valley-of-Hinnom etymology in
  commentary apparatus.

**The Greek-to-Ge'ez witness transition at ch 32:6** — the Akhmim
Panopolitanus codex breaks off mid-chapter at Raphael's identification
of the tree of wisdom as the Genesis 3 tree of knowledge. Chs 33-36
(the four-corners gates of heaven) survive in Ge'ez only.
