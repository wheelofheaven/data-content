**1 Enoch — Wheel of Heaven Translation. Ships A+B: the Book of the
Watchers (chs 1-36) + the Astronomical Book (chs 72-82).** The most
directly WoH-engaged text in the entire pseudepigrapha — named in
*Yes... I am Raelian* among the texts "closer to the truth,"
alongside the Mahābhārata, Kojiki, Popol Vuh, and the Epic of
Gilgamesh.

**47 chapters / 403 verses** across two compositions, produced from a
**per-verse layered witness reconstruction**:

- **Ship A — Book of the Watchers (chs 1-36; 248 verses).** Aramaic
  Dead Sea Scrolls fragments (**4Q201 / 4Q202 / 4Q204 / 4Q205 /
  4Q206 / 4Q207**; late 3rd c. BCE – 1st c. BCE) as PRIMARY witness
  where preserved; **Greek Akhmim Panopolitanus codex** (6th c. CE;
  chs 1-32 substantially complete) SECONDARY; **Ge'ez (Ethiopic)** —
  the only complete witness — TERTIARY and fallback. The Greek
  Akhmim breaks off mid-chapter 32; chs 33-36 are witnessed by Ge'ez
  alone.
- **Ship B — Astronomical Book / *Sefer ha-Mazzalot* / Book of the
  Luminaries (chs 72-82; 155 verses).** Aramaic DSS fragments
  **4Q208 / 4Q209 / 4Q210 / 4Q211** as PRIMARY witness where preserved
  — **4Q208 is the OLDEST surviving Enoch material overall**, dated by
  Milik 1976 to "the end of the third or beginning of the second
  century BCE," predating even the Watchers fragments. **No Greek
  witness** for chs 72-82 (Akhmim covers only chs 1-32); the layered
  schema collapses to Aramaic + Ge'ez. Significantly: **the Aramaic
  recension is materially longer than the Ge'ez**, with much
  astronomical detail (lunar synodic tables, gate-by-gate sun
  tracking, precise day-counts) abbreviated or omitted in the
  Ethiopic transmission tradition (Milik 1976; Nickelsburg–VanderKam
  2012; Drawnel 2011).

## Ship A — Book of the Watchers (chs 1-36)

Enoch's opening theophany prophecy (chs 1-5; the *Jude 14-15 quote
site* at 1:9); the Watchers' descent on Mount Hermon (ch 6) + the 200
sons-of-heaven taking human wives + the 20 Watcher chiefs (Šemiḥazah,
ʿAzazʾēl, Bărāqʾēl, Kōkabʾēl, etc.); the engendering of the Nephilim
giants (ch 7); **Asael's knowledge-transmission catalog** —
metallurgy, weapons-craft, cosmetics, magic, astronomical-divination
(ch 8); the archangels' intercession (ch 9); the Most High's
commission + Asael bound at Dudael (ch 10); **Enoch the
scribe-intercessor** (chs 12-13); **the throne-vision and the Great
Glory** (ch 14 — the four-tradition throne-vision cluster); the
indictment-sealed + the **giants-become-evil-spirits demonology**
(chs 15-16); Enoch's cosmic tours through the abyss + mountains-of-
fire + **the seven mountains of God** (chs 17-19, 24-25); the **list
of seven archangels** (ch 20; Aramaic 4 vs Greek 7 textual variant
preserved); the prison of the fallen stars (ch 21); **the four
hollow places of the dead** (ch 22 — foundational intermediate-state
doctrine); the **tree of life on the paradisiacal mountain** (chs
24-25); **Jerusalem as the center of the earth** (ch 26); **the
Valley of Hinnom = Gehenna** (ch 27 — fountainhead text for the 12
NT *geenna* references); the eastern paradise + aromatic trees (chs
28-31); **the tree of wisdom = Genesis 3 tree of knowledge** (ch 32 —
Sophia/Hokhmah fountainhead); the four-corners gates of heaven (chs
33-36; Ge'ez witness only).

## Ship B — Astronomical Book (chs 72-82)

**Ch 72** — **Uriel's prologue** + the sun's annual circuit through
the 6 eastern + 6 western gates. The central calendrical statement
at **72:32**: the sun completes 364 days "and nothing is added to
it" — foundational for the Qumran solar-calendar tradition.

**Chs 73-74** — the moon's nature; the **354-day lunar year vs the
364-day solar year**; the 10-day annual gap; multi-year reconciliation
tabulations. 4Q208 (the oldest Enoch fragment) preserves substantially
fuller fractional-light tables than Ge'ez records.

**Ch 75** — the four "leaders of thousands" governing the four 91-day
quarters of the year + the **four epact-days**. The sharpest polemic
verses: **"those who do not add them in — humans err"**.

**Ch 76** — the **twelve gates of the four winds** (3 per cardinal
direction); the differentiated effects of each (rains, snow, fog, dew,
beneficial vs destructive winds). Aramaic-primary at 76:1-2.

**Ch 77** — the four quarters of the earth; **"north as the dwelling
of the Most High"** (77:2; Aramaic-primary, extending the cosmic-
mountain cluster to Ps 48:3 *yarkəṯē ṣāp̄ôn* + Ugaritic Ṣapunu); the
seven mountains + seven rivers.

**Ch 78** — the names of sun and moon (corrupted in Ge'ez
transmission; preserved verbatim per layered-witness convention); the
moon's 14-day waxing + 14-day waning; **the 49-unit moonlight grid**
that 4Q208/4Q209 preserves but Ge'ez compresses.

**Ch 79** — Uriel's revelation summary.

**Ch 80** — **the cosmic-disorder oracle**. In the last days the
heavenly bodies will deviate, the moon will change its laws, rain
will fail, stars will transgress their orbits, and humans will mistake
stars for gods. **THE central NT cross-corpus chapter**:
{% libref(book="joel", chapter=2, verse=30) %}Joel 2:30-31{% end %}
→ {% libref(book="matthew", chapter=24, verse=29) %}Mt 24:29{% end %} /
{% libref(book="mark", chapter=13, verse=24) %}Mk 13:24-25{% end %} /
{% libref(book="luke", chapter=21, verse=25) %}Lk 21:25-26{% end %}
→ {% libref(book="revelation", chapter=6, verse=12) %}Rev 6:12-13{% end %}
→ Sura 81:1-14 + Sura 54:1. The apocryphal-tradition fountainhead
bridging Joel and the NT cosmic-signs cluster.

**Ch 81** — **the heavenly tablets** (*luḥōt ha-shamayim*) that
contain all deeds + all generations; Enoch instructed by **seven holy
ones**; the **one-year-with-his-children-then-final-assumption**
commission. Cross-corpus to Jubilees 5/23/30 + Dan 7:10 + Rev 5:1 +
Rev 20:12.

**Ch 82** — Enoch's instructions to **Methuselah**; the leaders of
the year's four seasons + 12 months + 360 days + 4 epact days. **The
364-day Qumran calendar polemic at its sharpest** — Aramaic-primary
at 82:4-5 from 4Q211. 4Q211 preserves Aramaic continuation BEYOND the
Ge'ez ending — confirming Milik's hypothesis that the original
Astronomical Book extended significantly further into autumn/winter
quarter-leader material that the Ge'ez tradition truncated.

## The Qumran 364-day-calendar polemic

The Astronomical Book is **the principal extant statement of the
Qumran community's 364-day solar calendar**, sharpest at 72:32 ("nothing
is added to it"), 75:2-3 ("humans err — they do not reckon the four
epact-days"), and 82:4-6 (the beatitude-polemic in Aramaic from
4Q211). The same calendar reappears in **Jubilees 6:32-38** (the
parallel polemical text) and is implemented in the **4Q319-4Q330
Mishmarot** calendrical texts, **CD 16:2-3** (calendar-as-covenant in
the Damascus Document), and **4QMMT** (the calendar as a mark of
identity-separation from mainstream Second Temple Judaism's
lunar-solar reckoning). Standard scholarship: VanderKam 1998
*Calendars in the Dead Sea Scrolls*; Beckwith 1996; Glessmer 1999;
Stern 2001.

## The Watchers myth (Ship A)

The 200 sons-of-heaven (*bnei elohim*; Aramaic *bn-ilm*; Greek
*huioi tou theou*) descend under Šemiḥazah to take human wives
(1 En 6-7) and beget the Nephilim — directly expanding
{% libref(book="genesis", chapter=6, verse=1) %}Genesis 6:1-4{% end %}
into a fully-developed mythological narrative. Asael (Aramaic
ʿAśāʾēl; Greek + Ge'ez Azazel) teaches humanity metallurgy, swords,
knives, shields, breastplates, mining, jewelry, cosmetics, alchemy,
and astrology (1 En 8:1-3) — **the canon's central Enoch citation
site**. The Watchers-as-Elohim-civilization reading and the
Asael-as-Elohim-technology-transfer reading are preserved as
canonical-reception phenomena at the **apparatus level only**
(glossary entries + per-verse commentary); the translation surface
preserves the scholarly mainstream reading (Reed 2005 *Fallen Angels*;
Nickelsburg 2001 Hermeneia; Stuckenbruck 2014). Same
acknowledgment-without-endorsement framework as the established
Anunnaki + Igigi + apkallū precedents in the Mesopotamian sub-corpus.

## Translation choices preserved

- **Per-verse layered witness attribution** across both Ships
- **Aramaic onomastics preserved** per Milik 1976: Šemiḥazah, ʿAśāʾēl,
  Bărāqʾēl, Kōkabʾēl, Mīkāʾēl, Śarīʾēl, Rāpāʾēl, Gabrīʾēl, ʾŪrīʾēl
- **Aramaic 4 vs Greek 7 archangels** textual variant preserved at
  ch 20
- **300-vs-3000 cubits giant-stature** variant preserved at ch 7:2
- *Watchers* renders ʿīrīn / *egrēgoroi* / *teguh* — standard
  scholarly English
- *Gehenna* at ch 27 with Valley-of-Hinnom etymology in apparatus
- **Calendrical-polemic voice preserved** at 72:32, 75:2-3, 82:4-6
- **18-vs-22-part day-ratio scholarly crux** at 72:7+ preserved
  without harmonization (Neugebauer 1985 two-system explanation in
  commentary)
- **Corrupted Ge'ez sun/moon names** at 78:1-2 preserved verbatim
  (no emendation)
