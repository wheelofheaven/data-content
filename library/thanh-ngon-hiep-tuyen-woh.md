**Thánh Ngôn Hiệp Tuyển** ("Compilation of Divine Messages"), Volume
One, gathers the dated séance messages received by the founders of
Đạo Cao Đài from Christmas 1925 onward — communications attributed to
the Supreme Being (Đức Chí Tôn) and other spirits, in the poetic and
prose forms characteristic of Caodai revelation. Volume One was
published by the church in 1927–1928 and is a foundational text of the
Third Universal Amnesty (*Tam Kỳ Phổ Độ*).

This entry carries the **original Vietnamese text**, transcribed from
the daotam.info witness. An in-house English translation through the
**Wheel of Heaven Translation Program** is forthcoming and will be
read here alongside the original.
