**Oomoto Shin'yu** (大本神諭, "Divine Revelations of Oomoto") is the
founding oracle of the Japanese new religion Oomoto — the automatic
writing transmitted through Deguchi Nao beginning in 1892, opening on
the famous line about the plum blossom that heralds the world's
renewal (*tatekae tatenaoshi*, "reconstruction and renewal"). It is
one of the two textual pillars of Oomoto, alongside Deguchi
Onisaburō's *Reikai Monogatari*.

This entry carries the **original Japanese text**, transcribed from
the reikaimonogatari.net witness. An in-house English translation
through the **Wheel of Heaven Translation Program** is forthcoming and
will be read here alongside the original.
