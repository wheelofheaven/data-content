**The Book of Abraham**
is part of the Restoration Scriptures collection in the Latter-day Saint Texts.

## About this edition

- **Coverage:** 6 chapters (5 text chapters plus the facsimile explanations), 139 verses
- **Reference format:** `ABR {chapter}:{verse}`
- **Translation:** Pearl of Great Price, 1913 Salt Lake City edition (ed. James E. Talmage), public domain
