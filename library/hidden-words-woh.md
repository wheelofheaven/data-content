**The Hidden Words** (*Kalimát-i-Maknúnih*) is a collection of short
aphorisms revealed by Bahá'u'lláh around 1858 during his exile in
Baghdad — 71 in Arabic (Part I) and 82 in Persian (Part II), each
opening with a vocative address ("O Son of Spirit…", "O ye that are
possessed of understanding and wisdom…"). It is among the most widely
read works of the Bahá'í canon.

This entry carries the **original Arabic and Persian text**, collated
against the authoritative Bahá'í World Centre edition. It is the
Bahá'í pilot of the **Wheel of Heaven Translation Program**: an
independent, in-house English translation is forthcoming and will be
read here alongside the original. It is unaffiliated with, and not a
substitute for, the authorised Bahá'í World Centre translations.
