**Jubilees Ship A: The Primeval + Patriarchal Frame (chs 1-12) —
Wheel of Heaven Translation.** The principal Hebrew-language
Enochian companion text + the most extensively cross-corpus-engaged
non-canonical text in Second Temple Jewish literature. **12 chapters
/ 373 verses**, from Moses on Sinai through Abraham's iconoclasm at
Ur.

**Per-verse layered witness reconstruction**: **Hebrew Dead Sea
Scrolls fragments** (4Q216 dated late 2nd c. BCE per Milik-VanderKam
DJD XIII 1994, with additional witnesses 4Q218 + 11Q12 across Ship A
scope) as PRIMARY where preserved; **Greek patristic citations**
(Epiphanius *De mensuris et ponderibus* 21-24 per Dean 1935)
SECONDARY where extant — notably the table-of-nations + sub-allotments
material at chs 8-9; **Ge'ez (Ethiopic)** — the only complete witness
— TERTIARY and fallback (VanderKam 1989 CSCO 510-511 + Charles 1902
PD).

Hebrew is the original composition language. Greek translation existed
but survives only in patristic fragments. **Hebrew DSS coverage of
Ship A scope is sparse** — only chs 1-2 (4Q216 substantial) + ch 2:26-27
(4Q218 Sabbath) + sparse 11Q12 fragments at chs 4 + 5:1 + (probably)
ch 8 have Hebrew witnesses; chs 3, 6-12 are Ge'ez-primary with sparse
Greek-secondary at chs 8-9.

## Narrative arc

**Ch 1** — Moses ascends Sinai for 40 days + 40 nights; God predicts
Israel's apostasy + restoration; **the heavenly tablets** are dictated
to Moses through **the angel of the presence** (*malak ha-panim*) —
the chief celestial mediator + the dictator of the entire book.

**Ch 2** — The angel of the presence recites the seven-days-of-creation
in the Genesis 1 pattern, with embedded Jubilees-distinctive
angelology (the **seven categories of angels** created on Day 1) and
proto-calendrical detail (the luminaries-for-signs formula at 2:9).
The Sabbath ordinance is established with the angel of the presence
keeping Sabbath in heaven alongside Israel.

**Ch 3** — Eden + the woman from the rib + Adam's naming of animals
+ the fall + the Jubilees-distinctive detail that **the serpent's
punishment includes loss of speech** ('their mouth was sealed... that
they no longer spoke'). The 80-day + 40-day post-birth purification
time-doublings are the foundational text for Lev 12. Adam burns
incense as **proto-priestly Adam**.

**Ch 4** — Adam's descendants through Cain + Awan + Azura to Noah.
**Jub 4:5** establishes the measure-for-measure principle ('by the
stone... his house fell upon him and he died'). **Jub 4:17-26 is THE
foundational Enoch-as-celestial-scribe text** — Enoch is the FIRST of
humanity to learn writing, composes a calendrical compendium, receives
apocalyptic-historical revelation, spends 6 jubilees of years with
the angels, writes the indictment of the Watchers, is taken into Eden,
and serves as celestial-priest + judge. **Jub 4:30** — the 1000-year-day
midrash on Gen 5:5 (cross-corpus to Ps 90:4 + 2 Pet 3:8).

**Ch 5** — The compressed **Watchers narrative** parallel to 1 Enoch
6-11 (Hebrew-primary at 5:1 via 11Q12). Jubilees uses *malake el*
'angels of God' rather than the Genesis 6:1 *bnei elohim* 'sons of
God' — a deliberate disambiguation. The giants slaughter each other
prior to the Flood (5:7-9). **Jub 5:13** — the heavenly tablets contain
all judgments in righteousness. **Jub 5:17-19** — the foundational Yom
Kippur etiology.

**Ch 6** — The **Noachic covenant** + the seven Noahide commands
(blood-prohibition + violence-prohibition; cross-corpus to b.
Sanhedrin 56a-60a + Acts 15). **Jub 6:17-22** is Jubilees' foundational
establishment of **Shavuot as the covenant-renewal feast**. **Jub
6:32-38 is THE FOUNDATIONAL 364-day-solar-calendar polemic**:
"observe the years according to this reckoning — 364 days, and they
will constitute a complete year" (6:32). The sharpest polemic
verse — 6:36 "they will make the moon disturb the seasons" — is the
defining polemical statement of the calendrical-identity-dispute that
separated the Qumran-Enochic-Jubilees community from the proto-Rabbinic
luni-solar tradition.

**Ch 7** — Noah's vineyard + drunkenness + curse of Ham/Canaan;
**Noah's testament** to his grandsons; the tripartite-giants nomenclature
(Naphidim + Nephilim + Elyo at 7:22); proto-Mastema material at 7:27.

**Chs 8-9** — **The table-of-nations + land-division-by-lot** under
angelic supervision. **Greek-primary at 8:12, 8:22, 8:25** (Epiphanius
Greek). **Jub 8:19 is THE EXPLICIT three-holy-mountains formula**:
**Mount Zion** as the navel-of-the-earth + **Mount Sinai** as the
center-of-the-desert + **Mount of the East / Eden-mountain** as the
center-of-the-world — extending the existing 7-tradition cosmic-mountain
cluster to an 8-tradition cluster. The 9:14-15 oath of binding
establishes the cosmographic-justification of the conquest-etiology.

**Ch 10** — **Mastema introduced** at 10:7-9 — the prince-of-the-spirits
petitions God to leave 1/10 of the demons unbound. One of the **earliest
extant proper-noun designations of the Satan-figure** in Second Temple
Judaism. **Noah's medicinal book** at 10:10-14 — the foundational text
for the **Sefer Refuot** tradition (cross-corpus to b. Pesachim 56a
where Hezekiah hides the Book of Cures). **The Tower of Babel** with
Jubilees-distinctive wind-overthrow detail (10:21 — "the Lord sent a
great wind against the tower and overthrew it onto the ground") +
seventy-languages dispersal (10:26).

**Ch 11** — Abram's birth + Mastema directs idolatry (11:3) + sends
ravens against Abram's seed (11:11; Abram drives them away by speaking
— foundational for the **Abram-as-child-iconoclast** tradition).

**Ch 12** — **Abraham's iconoclasm**. **Jub 12:12-14 is THE EARLIEST
EXTANT TEXT of the Abraham-iconoclasm-fire tradition**: Abram burns
down 'the house of idols' (12:12) + Haran dies trying to rescue the
idols (12:14). This is the seed of the trans-Abrahamic tradition
spanning Apocalypse of Abraham 1-8 + Genesis Rabbah 38:13 + b. Bava
Batra 91a (the rabbinic *Ur-Kasdim* as 'fire of the Chaldees' midrash)
+ Qur'an Sura 21:51-71 + 37:83-98 + 6:74-79 + 19:41-50 (Ibrahim cast
into the furnace). **Jub 12:19** — Abram's anti-Mastema prayer to El
Elyon. **Jub 12:25-26** is THE EARLIEST EXTANT TEXT of the **Hebrew-as-
creation-language tradition** — the angel opens Abram's mouth + ears
and teaches him Hebrew, "the language that had been from the beginning
of the world but had been forgotten" at Babel; Abram then reads the
pre-Mosaic Enochic + Noachic library.

## Cross-corpus density

Jubilees is the principal native-Hebrew witness to: the **Watchers
tradition** (parallel to 1 En 6-11); **Enoch-as-celestial-scribe** (Jub
4:17-26 — the foundational text for the Enoch-Metatron tradition);
the **heavenly tablets** motif (densest extant articulation, 25+
occurrences); the **giants-become-evil-spirits demonology** (Jub 10:1-6
parallel to 1 En 15:8); the **364-day solar calendar polemic** (Jub
6:32-38 foundational; 1 En 72-82 technical implementation).

**Ten major bidirectional wires** established or extended this pass:

1. **Mastema as the earliest extant Satan-figure** designation —
   bidirectional with CD 16:5 + 1QS 3:18-25 + 1QM 13:11 + Test. Levi
   19.
2. **Heavenly tablets densest extant articulation** — extends v2.67.0
   1 Enoch 81 entry; bidirectional to Dan 7:10 + Rev 5:1 + Rev 20:12.
3. **Enoch-as-scribe foundation** — Jub 4:17-26 the earliest systematic
   articulation; bidirectional to 1 En 12-13 + 3 Enoch / Metatron
   tradition.
4. **364-day calendar polemic foundation** — Jub 6:32-38 + Jub 6:36
   foundational polemic; CD 16:2-3 identifies Jubilees as calendrical
   authority.
5. **Abraham-iconoclasm-fire trans-Abrahamic tradition** — Jub 12:12-14
   the earliest extant text; bidirectional to Apoc. Abraham + Genesis
   Rabbah 38:13 + b. Bava Batra 91a + Qur'an Sura 21/37/6/19.
6. **Three-holy-mountains omphalos extension** — Jub 8:19 (Zion +
   Sinai + Mt-of-the-East) extends the cosmic-mountain cluster.
7. **Giants-become-evil-spirits demonology** — Jub 10:1-6 + 1 En
   15:8-12 bidirectional.
8. **Hebrew-as-creation-language tradition** — Jub 12:25-26 the earliest
   text; bidirectional to b. Sanhedrin 38b + Genesis Rabbah 18:4 +
   Jerome + Augustine.
9. **Tower-of-Babel wind-overthrow tradition** — Jub 10:21 the earliest
   specific wind-overthrow; bidirectional to Pseudo-Philo + Sib. Or.
   + 3 Bar + b. Sanhedrin 109a.
10. **Sefer Refuot / Noah's medicinal book** — Jub 10:10-14 the
    foundational text; bidirectional to b. Pesachim 56a + Aramaic Levi
    51-57 + Mesopotamian apkallū knowledge-transmission framework.

## Jubilees in the canon

Jubilees was widely read at Qumran — the 15+ DSS witnesses make it one
of the most-attested non-canonical texts in the Scrolls — and explicitly
cited at CD 16:2-3 (the Damascus Document) as authoritative on
calendrical questions. It is canonical in the **Ethiopian Orthodox
Tewahedo Church** (where it appears as *Kufale* 'division') and in the
**Beta Israel** (Ethiopian Jewish) tradition. It survived in these
canon-traditions but was lost to the wider Christian + rabbinic
traditions until the modern recovery.

## Translation choices preserved

- **Per-verse layered witness attribution** — Hebrew DSS primary where
  preserved; Greek Epiphanius secondary at chs 8-9; Ge'ez tertiary +
  fallback
- **Mastema** preserved as proper noun (not "Satan" / "the Adversary"
  on surface) — the Jubilees-specific identity is philologically distinct
  from later cosmic-dualist Satan-figure
- **Heavenly tablets** (*luhot ha-shamayim*) preserved as a fixed
  technical phrase across all 25+ occurrences
- **Angel of the presence** (*malak ha-panim*) preserved as a fixed
  Jubilees-distinctive title
- **Three holy mountains** trio preserved as proper nouns (Mount Zion
  + Mount Sinai + Mount of the East / Eden-mountain)
- *Watchers* / *malake el* / *bnei elohim* — "Watchers" / "angels of
  God" / "sons of God" per established 1 Enoch precedent
- **364-day-calendar polemical voice preserved** without softening or
  pre-resolution
- Mt-of-the-East identification ambiguity preserved at 8:19 (three
  competing scholarly identifications noted in commentary)
- Mastema vs Beliar variant at 1:20: principal Ge'ez Bēlhārā reading
  "Beliar" preserved
- 1/10 demon-bargain at 10:8-9: bargain-narrative voice preserved
  without harmonization with later cosmic-dualism
- NO archaic forms; NO Sitchin/WoH/ancient-astronaut framing on surface
- Acknowledgment-without-endorsement framework at the Mastema-as-rival-
  Elohim-faction reading + the Watchers-as-Elohim-civilization reading
  lives in commentary apparatus only
