**Enuma Elish** (Akkadian: 𒂊𒉡𒈠 𒂊𒇺 — "When on high")
is the Babylonian creation epic, the foundational text of
Mesopotamian cosmogony.

## About this edition

- **Coverage:** 8 sections (partial — OCR extraction from King's critical edition)
- **Translation:** L.W. King, *The Seven Tablets of Creation* (1902), public domain
- **Quality note:** This is an OCR extraction from Archive.org. Some tablets are
  incomplete and the Third Tablet is missing. The text quality will improve with
  future cleanup passes.
- **Reference format:** `EE {tablet}:{line}`
