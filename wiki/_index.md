# Encyclopedia

Brief, sourced entries for the terms, figures, places, and concepts that recur across the Wheel of Heaven corpus — Elohim, Yahweh, the Council, the precessional ages, the source texts the project reads from, and the comparative material the canon is read alongside.

Each entry carries a small badge labeling its main claim as **direct** (explicit in a primary source), **inferred** (reasonable reading of a source), or **speculative** (interpretive synthesis beyond what any single source states). Use the search and category filters to find what you need, or follow internal links from the timeline and library entries.
